About My Books

The Dancing Dinosaur

In this joyful picture book, Rex loves to dance—but the other dinosaurs just don’t get it!

“Why don’t you roar? Why don't you stomp?” they ask.

Rex has a simple answer: because dancing is what I love!

At first, the other dinosaurs think it’s strange… until Rex’s joyful moves inspire them to give it a try too. Soon, the whole jungle is shaking with a dino dance party!

A heartwarming story about staying true to yourself and celebrating what makes us all unique.
